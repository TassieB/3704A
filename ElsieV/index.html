Howard Gardner, ["8 Intelligences: Are You a Jack of All Trades or a Master of One?"](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oY2C4YgXm7I&ab_channel=BigThink), 1.5m views Howard Gardner tackles the idea that intelligence is not and should not be referred to as one single thing such as a test of IQ, but between 8 different intelligences. He belives that intelligence is multi-fascited and is measureable across these 8 fields: 1. Linguistic intelligence 2. Logical mathematic intelligence 3. musical intelligence 4. Spatial intelligence 5. Bodily kinetic intelligence 6. Interpersonal intelligence 7. Intrapersonal intelligence 8. Naturalist intelligence
