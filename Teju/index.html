Celebrity Scientist Summary


In this video, Noam Chomsky explains that advances in AI are useful for some purposes but not when it comes to understanding language processing in humans. One thing he mentioned that AI has achieved is structure dependence. He explains that humans do not have this capacity through an example of a study done with German speakers. In this study, German speakers were given two invented languages, one based on human language structure and one that is not. The particpant's brains were observed while reading this language. The results showed that while reading the language that violates language rules, brain patterns appeared as if they were solving a puzzle, not understanding language. Chomsky explained that this shows that the human brain is structured in a way where it expects certain things to be true; however, AI is able to achieve things that violate certain assumptions.
