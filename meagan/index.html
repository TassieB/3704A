Video Summary



In this video, Janelle Shane demonstrates that the way we train AI can greatly affect its performance. If we do not give it
give it clear instructions, it will tend to do something unexpected to solve the problem. One of the examples she gave was of an experiement
that asked an AI to go across an obstacle course (in a virtual environment). The expected outcome was that it would create a "body" and use its
legs to run across the course. But instead, it made itself into a giant tower, and when it falls down it can reach the end of the obstacle course.
Shane compared modern AI intelligence to a earthworm or less. Although AI can follow instructions well, it doesn't have an understanding of what it's doing
and what it should be doing. As a result, AI can can up with some weird solutions.
